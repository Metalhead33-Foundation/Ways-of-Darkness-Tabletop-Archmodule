\textbf{Requirement:} Granted automatically if the character is a Vampire or  a Theriantrope. Cannot be gained any other ways.

\textbf{Effect:} Acts as Basic Martial Arts if the character doesn't already have that feat. If the character does, then it simply gives a flat \Bonus{20\%} to unarmed damage of any kind.
