Instead, it means that the religion is either a monotheistic religion with a fire-god, or a polytheistic religion whose chief deity is a fire-god. \SoCalled{Fire cleanses the filth in this world.}}{Clerical Magic users can access Fire Magic, receiving the feat \textit{Basic Pyromancy} for free. The spellcaster also receives \textit{Basic Enchantment} for free.}{This virtue does not unlock any addition ways of gaining Divine Favour. Because of this, this virtue must be combined with an addition one that grants a way of gaining Divine Favour.}{Elementalism, Shamanism, Lunalatry, Heliolatry, Aerolatry, Geolatry, Hydrolatry}
