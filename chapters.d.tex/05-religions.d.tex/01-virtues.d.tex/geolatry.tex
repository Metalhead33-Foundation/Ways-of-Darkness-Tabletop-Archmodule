Instead, it means that the religion is either a monotheistic religion with a earth-god, or a polytheistic religion whose chief deity is a earth-god\Parentheses{dess} - usually some kind of Mother Earth. \SoCalled{May the earth nurture you.}}{Clerical Magic users can access Earth Magic, receiving the feat \textit{Basic Geomancy} for free. The spellcaster also receives \textit{Basic Geology} for free.}{This virtue does not unlock any addition ways of gaining Divine Favour. Because of this, this virtue must be combined with an addition one that grants a way of gaining Divine Favour.}{Shamanism, Lunalatry, Heliolatry, Aerolatry, Pyrolatry, Hydrolatry}
