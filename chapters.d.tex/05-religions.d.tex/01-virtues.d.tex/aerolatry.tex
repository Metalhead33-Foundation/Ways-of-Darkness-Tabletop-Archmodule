\ReligionRow{Aerolatry}{\textbf{Aerolatry} in its literal sense would mean worship of air - but no one simply worships the air we breathe. Instead, it means that the religion is either a monotheistic religion with a wind-god or thunder-god, or a polytheistic religion whose chief deity is a wind-god or thunder-god - usually some kind of Father Sky or Father Thunder. \SoCalled{Fly like the wind!}}{Clerical Magic users can access Air Magic, receiving the feat \textit{Basic Aeromancy} for free. The character also receives \textit{Basic Sneaking} for free.}{This virtue does not unlock any addition ways of gaining Divine Favour. Because of this, this virtue must be combined with an addition one that grants a way of gaining Divine Favour.}{Elementalism, Shamanism, Lunalatry, Heliolatry, Geolatry, Pyrolatry, Hydrolatry}
